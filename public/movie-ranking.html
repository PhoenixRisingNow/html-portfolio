The Best Movies According to Oksanna

My top 3 movies of all-time.

There Will Be Blood

Excellent story about an American Oil Tycoon.
Samuel Day Lewis plays the main character brilliantly and convincingly.
Trials, Tribulations and even murder sewn within the plot.

Shawshank Redemption

Fantastic movie starring Morgan Freeman.
A humanistic tail about the hardships of life.
It perfectly paints a realistic picture depicting human suffering and will power.

The Departed

Starring Jack Nicholson, Leonardo DiCaprio and Matt Damon.
A high action crime movie, full of suspense, tragic heroes and villians.
